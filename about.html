// origin.log — declassified

We believe AI should belong
to everyone. Not just the cloud.

DarkModeAI was built on a simple idea — powerful artificial intelligence shouldn't require a monthly subscription, an internet connection, or giving up your privacy to a corporation that profits from your data.

// the origin story

How it started.

// chapter 01 — the problem

AI exploded overnight. Suddenly everyone wanted access — students, small business owners, doctors, writers, everyday people. But the tools that existed required monthly fees, an internet connection, and handing over every word you typed to a corporation's server. That felt wrong.

// chapter 02 — the idea

What if AI could live on something as simple as a flash drive? Plug it in anywhere. No setup. No accounts. No internet. No data leaving your hands. Just intelligence — available to anyone, anywhere, at any time.

// chapter 03 — darkmode ai is born

We built it. Tested it. Refined it. And made it available to anyone who believes their data is their own business. Today DarkModeAI ships plug-and-play AI flash drives to everyday people, professionals, and businesses who refuse to compromise on privacy.
